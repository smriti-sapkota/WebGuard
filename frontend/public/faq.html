Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about scanning and usage.

What is WebGuard?

WebGuard is an automated web vulnerability scanner that detects SQL Injection, XSS, and CSRF vulnerabilities in web applications.

Do I need to log in to scan?

No. If your target app has protected pages, enable authentication in the scanner and provide valid credentials.

How long does a scan take?

Small applications usually complete quickly. Large targets may take longer depending on size and response time.

Can I scan any website?

Only scan targets that you own or have written permission to test.
